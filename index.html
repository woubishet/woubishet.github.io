10.10.2025 Recognized among the World’s Top 2% most-cited scientists by Stanford University ranking.

Driven by purpose and innovation, I am a civil engineer and AI researcher dedicated to advancing intelligent, resilient, and sustainable infrastructure. My work integrates civil engineering, data science, and artificial intelligence to bridge digital technologies with the built environment—transforming research into real-world impact.

Currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Center for Intelligent Infrastructures at Missouri S&T, I lead projects in AI-enabled structural modeling, materials intelligence, and digital twin technologies for smarter infrastructure systems. My research emphasizes practical impact and is supported by peer-reviewed publications in leading journals.

With global academic training and leadership experience in research management and large-scale infrastructure delivery, I bring a systems-level perspective, adaptability, and a strong commitment to mentorship and collaboration. I believe the future of infrastructure must be not only smart, but sustainable, inclusive, and human-centered—and I actively seek partnerships that drive meaningful, lasting change.
